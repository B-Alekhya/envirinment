Different Types of ENVIRONMENT

Natural Environment:

Planting trees is very important for the environment. The planting is beneficial to the ecosystem, and a better strategy to provide a nice atmosphere for the foreseeable future.Since urbanisation and industrialisation are continuously expanding, and leading to pollution, it is crucial to begin planting trees in urban and suburban areas. Moreover, trees filter the air we breathe and reduce pollution levels. As an illustration, the government of Delhi planted several trees in the urban areas to reduce pollution, and after a suitable amount of time, the city's residents noted a marked improvement in the environment. Therefore, planting not only aids in keeping the environment clean but also maintains people's health.
